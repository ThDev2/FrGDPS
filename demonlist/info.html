About FrGDPS Demonlist

Community platform to categorize and rank the hardest demon levels in FrGDPS

What is Demonlist?

Demonlist is a list of the hardest demon levels in Geometry Dash sorted by difficulty. Unlike the official in-game level list, the demonlist is created and maintained by the player community.

FrGDPS Demonlist focuses specifically on demon levels created for FrGDPS private servers, with an independent ranking and verification system.

Difficulty System

We use a demon classification system with 5 main difficulty levels.:

Easy Demon

Beginner level demons, suitable for those who are just starting to play demons. Example: The Nightmare, The Lightning Road

Medium Demon

Medium difficulty demons, require a solid foundation. Example: B, Platinum Adventure

Hard Demon

Challenging demons with complex gameplay. Example: Nine Circles, Jawbreaker

Insane Demon

Demons are very difficult, only for experienced players. Example: Windy Landscape, Supersonic

Extreme Demon

The highest level, only the hardest levels fall into this category. Example: tertinggi, hanya level terberat yang masuk kategori ini. Contoh: Bloodbath, Zodiac

FAQ

Q: How to submit level to demonlist?
A: Use the submit page on this website by completing all the required information.

Q: What are the level requirements to enter the demonlist?
A: Level must be demon, have video verification, and pass the assessment of our verification team.

Q: How does the ranking system work?
A: Rankings are based on a combination of technical difficulty, level length, and community consensus.
